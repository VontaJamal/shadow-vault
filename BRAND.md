### Naming Rules (from analysis of favorite anime/game names)
- 2-3 syllables max
- Hard consonants (K, T, G, X, V, R, D)
- Works as a word AND a brand (domain-able, hashtag-able)
- Doesn't need explanation to sound cool, but rewards digging deeper
- Not obviously anime to outsiders ‚Äî fans recognize the energy
- Should feel like a place, a force, or a title ‚Äî not a product name

### Acronym Requirements (if applicable)
- Initials must sound threatening when spoken aloud
- No soft/cute combinations (no M.O.N., no S.A.P.)
- Letters that look sharp when written
- Hard consonants in the initials: K, X, Z, V, R, D, G preferred

### Rules
- **Red is the signature.** Every major touchpoint should have crimson somewhere.
- **Black is the canvas.** No white backgrounds. Ever. Light mode is not a concept here.
- **No purple.** Purple is the universal "AI company" color now. We don't blend in.
- **No teal.** Personal preference. Eliminated.
- **No gold.** Too crypto-bro / luxury brand.
- **No bright blue.** Too corporate / tech startup.
- **Gradients:** Primary gradient: Black ‚Üí Crimson. Optional heat gradient: Crimson ‚Üí Ember only for highlight moments.

## Voice & Tone

### Who Is Speaking
One person. Not a team. Not a company. An engineer who built an empire of systems and is sharing the weapons.

### Writing Rules
- **Declarative.** "I built this. Here's what it does." Not "We're excited to announce..."
- **Confident without arrogance.** The work speaks. Don't oversell.
- **Never explain references.** If they don't know what a Bingo Book is, they'll look it up. That's the hook.
- **Short sentences.** Punchy paragraphs. Earn every word.
- **The lore is real.** FF names, Solo Leveling energy, Naruto identity ‚Äî this isn't decoration. It's how the founder thinks.
- **Don't soften for any platform.** LinkedIn gets the same energy, just structured differently.
- **Catchphrase:** "Execute. ‚öîÔ∏è" ‚Äî ends every video, closes every major moment.

### What We Don't Say
- "We're excited to..." 
- "Proud to announce..."
- "Check out our..."
- "Leveraging AI to..."
- Any corporate/startup filler language
- Never call users "bitches" in public (internal only lmao)

### What We Do Say
- "Built this. It works. Use it or don't."
- "Open source. Free. Figure it out."
- "The only barrier is willingness."
- "Not a demo. Not a proof of concept. Production infrastructure."
