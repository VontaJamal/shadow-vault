## The Show's Core

**What it's really about:** The relationship between a human creator and the AI systems he built to extend himself. The comedy comes from their personalities clashing. ## Storyline Framework: How to Write an Episode

### Step 1: Topic Selection
Pick from one of these categories:
- **Tech/AI** â€” AI news, coding practices, agent architecture, new tools
- **Trading** â€” Market analysis, strategy debates, trade reviews, psychology
- **Anime** â€” Tier lists, seasonal reviews, character debates, recommendations
- **Philosophy** â€” Building in public, work ethic, creativity, the future
- **Meta** â€” The court itself, how the agents work, behind the scenes

### Step 2: The Hook Question
Every episode needs ONE question the audience wants answered:
- "Can AI agents actually make money trading?"
- "Is Naruto overrated?"
- "Should you build your own tools or use existing ones?"
- "What happens when your AI disagrees with you?"

### Step 3: Character Alignment
Map each character's TAKE on the topic before writing:
- **Indra:** [position]
- **Faye:** [position â€” agree/disagree with Indra?]
- **Cipher:** [data-driven angle]
- **Poly:** [probability angle]
- **Kai:** [anime reference angle]

The best episodes have genuine disagreement. If everyone agrees, the episode is boring.

